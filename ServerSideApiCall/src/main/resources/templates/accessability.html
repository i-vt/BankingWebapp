Accessibility Statement

1. Our Commitment

Province 701 Financial is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We strive to ensure that our services are available to everyone, including individuals with disabilities, and we actively work to enhance the accessibility and usability of our website.

2. Web Accessibility Standards

Our website has been designed and developed with accessibility in mind, and we aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines outline best practices for making web content accessible to users with a variety of disabilities, including those with visual, auditory, cognitive, and motor impairments.

  • Keyboard navigation to ensure that all website functions can be accessed using a keyboard.
  • Consistent and simple layouts to enhance readability and usability.
  • Scalable text that can be resized without loss of functionality or content.
  • Accessible forms with appropriate labels, instructions, and error messages.

4. Ongoing Efforts

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website and services. Our development and design teams are trained in accessibility best practices, and we regularly review our website to identify and resolve accessibility issues.

We are also committed to making any necessary improvements or adjustments in response to feedback from our users and advances in web accessibility standards.

5. Third-Party Content

While we aim to ensure that all content on our website meets accessibility standards, there may be some third-party content, such as videos, links, or embedded elements, that are not fully accessible. We encourage third-party providers to offer accessible content, and we are working to ensure that external resources we use are as accessible as possible.

6. Browser and Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with commonly used browsers and assistive technologies, including screen readers. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser and ensuring that assistive technologies are up-to-date.

We regularly test our website with various browsers and assistive technologies to ensure a smooth user experience for all users.

7. Contact Us for Accessibility Support

If you encounter any difficulties accessing our website or have suggestions on how we can improve accessibility, please contact us. We take accessibility seriously, and we welcome feedback to help us continually improve.

You can reach us at accessibility@province701financial.com or call us at 1-800-123-4567. Our customer support team is available to assist with accessibility inquiries.

8. Alternative Formats

If you require any content on our website in an alternative format (such as large print, braille, or audio), please contact us, and we will provide the information in the format that best meets your needs.
